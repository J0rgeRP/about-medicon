Privacy Policy

Transparency and security for your health data.

Thank you for using Medicon. I am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how my application handles your information.

No Data Collection

Medicon is a purely offline application. I do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, health information, or usage statistics on external servers. I do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Local Storage Only

All information you enter into the application is stored locally on your device using a secure Room database. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to export it.

No Permissions Required

To ensure your privacy and security, Medicon does not request any dangerous permissions.

  • No Internet Access: The app cannot connect to the internet.
  • No Location Tracking: I do not track your location.
  • No Camera/Microphone: I do not access your sensors.

Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete all stored information at any time by clearing the application data in your device settings or by uninstalling the application. You can also export your data to CSV format for your own records or backups.
